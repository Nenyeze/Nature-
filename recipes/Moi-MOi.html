MOI-MOI

Main Ingredients

  • Beans (Black-eye, or Brown Beans)
  • Red fresh pepper
  • salt
  • a spoon of oil(Red or Vegetable)
  • crayfish
  • Egg(optional)
  • shombo
  • Grinder or Blender

    • STEPS

    1. wash about 2cups of Beans, sieve and remove the back
    2. wash your fresh pepper, crayfish and shombo
    3. put your wash beans in the blender, add little water
    4. add your washed fresh pepper, shombo, crayfish to the beans in blender
    5. blend to smooth
